School Environment

Parevartan School, within its resources, makes every possible effort to meet the individual needs on their path to spiritual, academic, physical and social development.

This development ensues in educational opportunities that are both curricular and co-curricular. As a result, the admission policies and procedures are such that individuals seeking admission to Parevartan School are able to demonstrate their potential for success in the curricular and co-curricular opportunities.
